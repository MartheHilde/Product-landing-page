Discover Whiskas

WHISKAS® has been making food for cats since 1958 and we use this experience in everything we do. Not only are we committed to developing delicious products, we’re devoted to helping all cats live healthy and happy lives.
WHISKAS® is there for the whole journey – as your cat grows with us, we are constantly looking for ways to improve our nutrition and care.
As the makers of WHISKAS® we know and understand how much people love their cats – we feel the same. We love them for their independent spirits but understand they still need love and care.
